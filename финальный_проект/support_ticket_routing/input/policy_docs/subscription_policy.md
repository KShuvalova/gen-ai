# Subscription policy

Subscription tickets include unexpected cancellation, cancellation requests, renewal problems, plan upgrade, plan downgrade, trial expiration, and unclear subscription status.

Typical evidence phrases:
- subscription was cancelled
- without my request
- cancel subscription
- renewal
- plan
- upgrade
- downgrade
- trial

Default department: support_l2.

Recommended actions:
1. Check the current subscription status.
2. Verify whether cancellation or renewal was initiated by the customer or by the system.
3. If the customer reports cancellation without request, review account activity and billing events.
4. If the ticket is connected to payment, billing_team may be consulted, but the main route remains support_l2 unless the issue is primarily a payment failure.
5. Escalate if the customer is blocked from using a paid plan or if SLA is breached.

The system must not promise a refund unless the ticket clearly asks for refund or billing policy applies.
